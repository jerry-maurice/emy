About us

Emmanuel Maranatha Youth, "EMY", is a prayer group whose goal is to raise up a legacy of young people who has the capacity to learn, to follow the Word of God and to be empowered by the Holy Spirt who will help them understand and recognize who Jesus is. He will also help them fulfill the real purpose of their calling. Our objective is to respond to the Social and Spiritual needs of the youth by providing relative programs, activities, and events that will allow them to build a strong foundation of faith that will enable them to express and share their faith and to use their talent in order to grow as children of God
